# Privacy Policy for Kablast

**Effective date:** 2026-06-14

Kablast helps users review and clean files on their Android device.

We do not sell personal data.

The app may process files and storage information locally on the device to show cleanup suggestions. Files are not uploaded to our servers.

If ads or analytics are added, third-party providers may collect device identifiers, usage data, and advertising-related data according to their own privacy policies.

Users can choose which files to delete. Nothing is deleted without user confirmation.
